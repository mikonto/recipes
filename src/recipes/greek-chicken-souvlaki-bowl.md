title: Greek Chicken Souvlaki Bowl
description: Complete Greek meal with chicken meatballs, roasted potatoes, fresh salad, and tzatziki
prepTime: 20 min
cookTime: 35 min
servings: 2
tags:
  - recipes
  - greek
  - chicken

ingredientSections:
  - name: "For the Chicken Meatballs"
    items:
      - 400g ground chicken
      - 2 cloves garlic, minced
      - 1 tsp dried oregano
      - 1/2 tsp ground cumin (optional)
      - Salt and pepper to taste
      - 2 tbsp lemon juice
      - 1 tbsp olive oil
  - name: "For the Roasted Potatoes"
    items:
      - 400g potatoes, cut into 2cm cubes
      - 2 tbsp olive oil
      - 1 tsp dried oregano
      - Salt to taste
  - name: "For the Greek Salad"
    items:
      - 2 medium tomatoes, cut into chunks
      - 1/2 cucumber, diced
      - 100g feta cheese (Puck), cubed
      - 50g black olives
      - 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
      - 2 tbsp olive oil
      - 1 tbsp lemon juice (optional)
      - Fresh mint and basil, chopped
  - name: "For the Tzatziki"
    items:
      - 200g Greek yogurt
      - 1-2 tsp Eridanous tzatziki spice mix
      - 1/4 cucumber (75-100g), grated
      - 1 tbsp olive oil
      - Optional squeeze of lemon juice
instructionSections:
  - name: "Preparation"
    startNumber: 1
    steps:
      - "Preheat oven to 200째C."
      - "Start potatoes: Toss potato cubes (400g) with olive oil (2 tbsp), oregano (1 tsp), and salt. Spread on one side of a large baking sheet. Put in oven."
  - name: "Make the Tzatziki"
    startNumber: 3
    steps:
      - "Grate cucumber (1/4 cucumber) and squeeze out all water using a kitchen towel."
      - "Mix yogurt (200g) with tzatziki spice mix (1 tsp), taste, add more if desired."
      - "Stir in squeezed cucumber and olive oil (1 tbsp). Refrigerate until serving."
  - name: "Prepare and Cook the Meatballs"
    startNumber: 6
    steps:
      - "Mix ground chicken (400g) with garlic (2 cloves), oregano (1 tsp), cumin (1/2 tsp), salt, pepper, and lemon juice (2 tbsp)."
      - "Form into 8-10 meatballs. Drizzle with olive oil (1 tbsp)."
      - "After 15 minutes: Add meatballs to the baking sheet with potatoes."
      - "Bake together for 15-20 minutes more, turning meatballs once. Potatoes should be golden (30-35 mins total), meatballs cooked through (74째C internal)."
  - name: "Make the Greek Salad and Assemble"
    startNumber: 10
    steps:
      - "While everything bakes: Combine tomatoes (2), cucumber (1/2), feta cubes (100g), olives (50g), and onion (1/4)."
      - "Drizzle with olive oil (2 tbsp) and lemon if using. Add fresh herbs."
      - "Assembly: Divide roasted potatoes between bowls. Add Greek salad and meatballs. Top with generous tzatziki and extra fresh herbs."
notes: |
  **Tips:**
  - For crispier potatoes: start at 220째C, reduce to 200째C when adding meatballs
  - The tzatziki spice mix already contains salt, so taste before adding more
  - Save any extra tzatziki for tomorrow - it keeps 2-3 days
