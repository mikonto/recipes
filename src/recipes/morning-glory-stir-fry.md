title: Morning Glory Stir Fry
description: Morning Glory Stir Fry is a delicious side dish that can be ready in less than 15 minutes. This simple vegetable side dish is rich in flavors and goes wonderfully with steamed rice.
prepTime: 5 min
cookTime: 5 min
servings: 2-3
tags:
  - recipes
  - thai
  - asian
  - stir-fry
  - quick
  - side-dish
  - vegetables
ingredients:
  - 350g morning glory/water spinach, cut into 10cm pieces
  - 3 shallots, thinly sliced
  - 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  - 3 curly red chilis, diagonally sliced
  - 80ml hot water
  - 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  - 1/2 teaspoon salt
  - 1/4 teaspoon granulated sugar
  - 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
instructions:
  - "Stir-fry spices: In a large frying pan, heat oil (1 tablespoon) on medium-high heat. Once the oil is heated, add shallots (3, thinly sliced), garlic (2 cloves, thinly sliced), and curly red chilis (3, diagonally sliced). Stir fry until fragrant for about 1-2 minutes."
  - "Add morning glory (350g) and hot water (80ml). Stir and cook until the morning glory is wilted."
  - "Add seasonings: Add oyster sauce (2 tablespoons), salt (1/2 teaspoon), and granulated sugar (1/4 teaspoon). Taste and adjust the seasonings. Turn off the heat and serve."
notes: For a gluten-free version, use oyster sauce labeled gluten-free.
